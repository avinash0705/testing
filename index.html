    Job Description

    • Develop and optimize chemical processes for the production of foam and ABC powder, taking into account safety, cost, and efficiency.
    • Conduct experiments and analyze data to identify and solve problems in the production process.
    • Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to foam and ABC powder production, such as quality control problems or equipment malfunctions.
    • Monitor and analyze production data to identify opportunities for process improvements and cost reduction.
    • Collaborate with suppliers and vendors to source raw materials and equipment for foam and ABC powder production.


    What we need in you to be successfull in this role :

    • Good understanding of the fundamentals of chemical engineering including, Surface tension , Film formation , material and fluid dynamics, heat transfer, thermodynamics and mixing phenomena
    • Capable to identify new methods of manufacturing chemicals and creating the process.
    • Capable to prepare MSDS and technical datasheet of the foam concentrates.
    • Strong knowledge on UL 162, UL 139 , EN 1568 and ICAO foam concentrate testing requirements and ISO 17025


    Desired Candidate Profile

    • Bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering or related field.
    • Strong knowledge of chemical engineering principles and processes.
    • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
    • Physical ability to work in a laboratory or production environment.
    • Ability to manage multiple projects and prioritize tasks effectively.
    • Effective communication and collaboration skills to work with cross-functional teams.
